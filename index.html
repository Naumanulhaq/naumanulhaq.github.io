About Me

Professional Overview

I am a Remote Sensing and GIS Specialist with over 10 years of experience applying Earth observation and geospatial analytics to agriculture, climate resilience, disaster risk reduction, and natural resource management. Currently at FAO Pakistan, I develop geospatial methodologies, support national partners, and lead analyses that guide evidence-based decisions.

My work ranges from land cover mapping, crop type monitoring, and crop health monitoring to geospatial flood impact assessment, forest cover change detection, climate pattern assessments, sampling design, and multi-criteria analysis. I regularly use tools like Python, Google Earth Engine, Earth Map, EO dashboards, and ArcGIS Pro for scalable, automated workflows.

I focus on creating simple, usable, and actionable products for governments, development partners, and field teams—whether spatial models, dashboards, maps, or technical briefs. I translate complex datasets into insights that improve planning, monitoring, and emergency interventions, and I'm passionate about integrating remote sensing with data innovation, capacity development, and digital transformation for agriculture and food systems.
