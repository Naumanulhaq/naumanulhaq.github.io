About Me .

I am a Remote Sensing and GIS Specialist with over 10 years of experience applying Earth observation and geospatial analytics to critical sectors like agriculture, climate resilience, disaster risk reduction, and natural resource management.

My expertise lies in leading the development of geospatial methodologies, managing multi-stakeholder workflows, and providing strategic guidance to national partners and field teams. From land cover mapping, crop type monitoring, and yield estimation to flood impact assessments and climate pattern analysis, I deliver actionable spatial products such as interactive dashboards and spatial models that support evidence-based decision-making.

I have extensively collaborated with major international development organizations including UNFAO, ADB, and USAID, translating complex geospatial datasets into practical insights for policy operations.
